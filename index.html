About

I am an active member and volunteer within the Dallas design community with organizations such as the Big Design Conference, M3+ UX Mentorship Group, Circles Design Conference, and AIGA Dallas. Over the past 3 years, I have attended over 100 design-related events in the Dallas community!

Most recently, I completed UX/UI Design work for Policy Angel, a startup that simplifies how property owners discover grants, store documentation, locate contractors, and manage insurance policies.

I have given back to the college design community, serving as a judge for multiple UX/UI design competitions, hosting design-related workshops, and giving advice to student designers. I previously served as the president for AIGA UT Dallas and as the design lead for the UX Club at UT Dallas.

I graduated from the University of Texas at Dallas in 2024 with a Bachelor’s Degree in Design & Media Arts and a concentration in User Experience Design, and I have experience in a wide range of fields including human computer interaction, user research, graphic design, programming, advertising, and more.
