About

I am an active member and volunteer within the Dallas design community, including organizations such as the Big Design Conference, M3+ UX Mentorship Group, Circles Design Conference, and AIGA Dallas. Over the past 3 years, I have attended over 100 design-related events in the Dallas community!

Most recently, I worked as a UX/UI Designer at Policy Angel, a startup that simplifies how property owners discover grants, store documentation, locate contractors, and manage insurance policies.

I have also given back to the college design community, serving as a judge for multiple UX/UI design competitions, hosting design-related workshops, and connecting with new designers. I previously served as President for the AIGA UT Dallas chapter and as the Design Lead for the UX Club at UT Dallas.

I graduated from the University of Texas at Dallas in 2024 with a Bachelor’s Degree in Design & Media Arts and a concentration in User Experience Design, and I have experience in a wide range of fields including Human Computer Interaction, User Research, Graphic Design, Programming, Advertising, and more.
